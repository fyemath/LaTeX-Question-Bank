Write the rational exponent as radical expression (assume all variables are positive).
\[
  \varx^{\reducedfraction{\a}{\m}}.
\]

\begin{solution}
\[
  \varx^{\reducedfraction{\a}{\m}}
= 
  \ifnum\m=2
    \sqrt{\varx^{\invisibleoneinpower{\a}}}
  \else
      \sqrt[\m]{\varx^{\invisibleoneinpower{\a}}}
  \fi
.
\]

\end{solution}
