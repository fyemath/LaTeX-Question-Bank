\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\p}{random(1,5)}
\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\P}{1000*\p}
\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\r}{random(3,9)}
\pgfmathsetmacro{\R}{\r/100}
\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\t}{random(4,8)}
\pgfmathsetmacro{\ans}{int(\P*exp(\R*\t))}

Use the formula $\displaystyle A=P\cdot e^{rt}$ to find the balance of an investment $\$\P$  at  $\r\%$ compounded continuously after $\t$ years. Round your answer to nearst dollars.

\begin{solution}
\[A(\t)=\P e^{\R \cdot \t}\approx \ans.\]
\end{solution}
