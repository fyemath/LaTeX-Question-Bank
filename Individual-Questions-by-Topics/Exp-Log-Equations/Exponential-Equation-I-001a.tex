The point $(x, 18)$ is on the graph of the function $f(x)=2^{2x+1}$. Find the value of $x$.  Round your result to the nearest thousandth.

\begin{solution}
\[
\begin{split}
2^{2x+1}&=18\\
2x+1&=\log_2(18)\\
2x&=\log_2(18)-1\\
x&=\frac{1}{2}(\log_2(18)-1)\approx1.585
\end{split}
\]
\end{solution}
