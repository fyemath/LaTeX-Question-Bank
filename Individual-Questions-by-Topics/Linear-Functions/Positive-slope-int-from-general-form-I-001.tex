\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\b}{random(1,4)} 

\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\n}{random(1,3)}


\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\a}{\b*\n} 


\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\yint}{random(1,5)-3} 

\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\yintabs}{abs(\yint)} 

\pgfmathtruncatemacro{\c}{\b*\yint}


% \pgfmathtruncatemacro{\yintsign}{\a*\c}
 




Find the slope-intercept form equation of the line defined by the equation 
\[\a x - {\ifnum\b=1 \else \b \fi} y =\c.\]


\begin{solution}
To find the slope-intercept form, we solve for $y$ from the given equation.

\[
\begin{split}
		\a x - {\ifnum\b=1 \else \b \fi} y = & \c\\
		\ifnum\b=1  
			-y 
		\else
			-\b y
		\fi
			= & -\a x+
						\ifnum\c<0
							(\c)
						\else 
							\c
						\fi
		\\
		y   =  & \invisibleoneincoef{\n} x
						\ifnum\yint<0
							+ \yintabs.
						\else 
							- \yint.
						\fi
\end{split}
\]
\end{solution}
