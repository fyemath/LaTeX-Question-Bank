Factor completely the following polynomial.
\[\invisibleoneincoef{\acube}\varx^3  + \bcube.\]


\begin{solution}
\[\begin{split}
&\invisibleoneincoef{\acube}\varx^3  + \bcube \\
=&(\invisibleoneincoef{\a}\varx)^3  + (\b)^3\\
=&(\invisibleoneincoef{\a}\varx  + \b)(\invisibleoneincoef{\asqr}\varx^2 - \ab\varx+\bsqr)
\end{split}
\]
\end{solution}
